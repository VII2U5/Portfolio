About

Craft rooted in
dark anatomy

I build 3D characters and creatures — from initial blockout through rigging and engine integration. My work sits at the intersection of biological plausibility and something deliberately wrong, influenced by the visual logic of Giger, Beksiński, and the mechanical mysticism in FromSoftware's art direction.

Currently completing my diploma project: a full production pipeline for an original 3D character, covering modelling, rigging, and integration into Unreal Engine 5. Alongside this I maintain personal creature lore systems and mod development for ARK Survival Ascended.

Available for commissions and collaborative projects. Interested in game studios, animated series production, and creature design pipelines.
